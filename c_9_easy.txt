Mīlitēs Rōmānī perturbātī sunt. Caesar tulit auxilium in tempore opportūnissimō. Hostēs cōnstitērunt et Caesar redūxit legiōnēs in castram. Tunc erant diēs tempestātēs, quae et nostrōs in castrīs continērent et hostem ā pugnā prohibērent. Interim barbarī nūntiōs in omnēs partēs dīmīsērunt. Barbarī voluērunt expellere Rōmānōs.

Post diēs tempestātēs, Caesar dūxit XXX equitēs et aliōs mīlitēs et proelium fēcit contrā hostem.
Eōdem diē lēgātī ab hostibus missī ad Caesarem dē pāce vēnērunt.
